10

Philosophy

Where a habit app gamifies, Bushido OS dignifies. There are no points, because you are not playing. There is a seal, because you are signing your name to a day.

Discipline is a design problem. Calm paper lowers the cost of showing up; one vermilion action removes deliberation; mono numbers that never flatter build the self-trust that streaks are made of. Every choice has a long-term consequence — the design’s choice is restraint, and its consequence is trust.
